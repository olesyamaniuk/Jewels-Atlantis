Game Description

Embark on a Jewel-Filled Adventure Under the Sea

Welcome to Jewels Atlantis: Puzzle Game, where the legendary lost city comes to life through dazzling puzzles and breathtaking visuals. As an adventurer, your mission is to restore Atlantis to its former glory by solving intricate jewel puzzles and unveiling hidden treasures.

  • Storyline:

    Long ago, Atlantis was a thriving civilization rich with magic and treasures. But it sank into the depths of the ocean, hidden for centuries. Now, you hold the key to revive its beauty. Match jewels, complete challenging quests, and unlock ancient secrets to bring Atlantis back to life.

  • Gameplay Mechanics:

    Match 3 or more jewels of the same color to create combos and unlock magical effects.
    Solve increasingly complex puzzles to progress through different underwater landscapes.
    Use power-ups like the Trident Blast and Ocean Wave to clear obstacles and earn bonuses.
    Collect rare treasures and artifacts hidden within each level to rebuild Atlantis.
    Explore themed chapters, such as Coral Gardens, The Pearl Chamber, and The Sunken Palace.

Game Atmosphere

Immerse yourself in an enchanting underwater world where every detail feels alive. From the luminescent glow of deep-sea jewels to the haunting beauty of sunken temples, Jewels Atlantis creates an experience that’s as magical as it is captivating. Hear the gentle hum of ocean currents, the chime of matching jewels, and the call of ancient Atlantean spirits guiding your journey.
